Sunset Wine Tasting Cruise

Experience a relaxing and atmospheric sunset cruise aboard the VillaVis boat. We depart from the VillaVis dock and head out to sea while cruising along the beautiful coastline of Curaçao.

During the sunset you will enjoy an exclusive wine tasting with a selection of wines and delicious matching appetizers. A perfect experience for a romantic evening, a fun outing with friends, or a special occasion.

Duration: approx. 3 hours.
Price: upon request.
Minimum: 6 persons.
