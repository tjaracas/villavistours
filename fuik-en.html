Fuik Bay Day Trip

Fuik Bay is one of Curaçao's most beautiful and peaceful bays, only accessible by boat. Enjoy the turquoise waters, relax on board, and take a dip with our provided snorkel gear. Drinks and light snacks are included. This trip is all about unwinding and soaking up the island vibes.

Duration: 3 hours – $85 per person
